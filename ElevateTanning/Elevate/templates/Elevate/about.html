About Elevate

Rachelle is a professional spray tan artist and law student based in Vancouver, BC. She is passionate about sunless tanning as it provides women with a safer alternative to traditional tanning methods, such as tanning beds. Living in a city with rainy and cold weather, she also understands the desire to maintain a sun-kissed glow year-round.

As someone who loves tanning herself, Rachelle knows the confidence boost it can give and wants to make it as affordable as possible so everyone, especially students, can achieve that luxurious post-spray tan feeling and glow. She loves giving women the confidence boost that comes with a fresh spray tan, and her goal is to show through her work that spray tans can elevate the appearance of every body and skin tone. She offers organic custom spray tans with a wide variety of shade options, which are all very natural; just like foundation for your skin!

Rachelle is grateful for the opportunity to share her passion, work in the beauty industry, and connect with all kinds of different women and people.
